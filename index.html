Technical Capabilities

Detailed analytical services using state-of-the-art laboratory equipment.

High-Capacity Universal Testing

Equipped to handle extreme industrial loads, our massive floor-standing FIE Universal Testing Machine pushes heavy-duty construction materials and industrial metals to their absolute breaking points to ensure unparalleled structural safety.

  • High-Tonnage Tensile Testing
  • Heavy Industrial Compression Analysis
  • Proof Stress & Yield Point Evaluation
  • Transverse & Bending Strength Verification

Digital Data Acquisition

Raw power requires absolute precision. Our testing bays are connected to advanced computerized consoles that capture micro-second data points during material failure, generating automated, highly accurate stress-strain analytics.

  • Real-Time Load & Displacement Tracking
  • Automated Stress-Strain Curve Generation
  • Digital Yield & Elongation Reporting
  • NABL-Compliant Data Logging

Hardness Testing

Material durability and wear resistance are paramount in engineering. Featuring our dedicated Rockwell hardness testing apparatus, we accurately evaluate the resistance of your metals to permanent indentation and localized deformation.

  • Rockwell Hardness Evaluation
  • Brinell Hardness Testing
  • Surface Durability Verification

Microstructural Analysis

Imperfections aren't always visible to the naked eye. Our high-magnification digital optical microscopes allow our metallurgists to examine grain structures, identify phases, and detect microscopic inclusions within metal alloys.

  • Grain Size Measurement
  • Micro-flaw & Crack Detection
  • Phase & Alloy Composition Verification

Impact Analysis

How does your material react to a sudden, violent collision? Our dedicated FIE pendulum impact testing bay determines the energy absorption characteristics and fracture toughness of materials under extreme, sudden stress.

  • Charpy V-Notch Testing
  • Izod Impact Testing
  • Fracture Toughness Evaluation

Precision Sample Preparation

Accurate diagnostics start with absolutely flawless samples. Our preparation bay utilizes advanced SUN-TECH polishing and grinding machinery to prepare metal cross-sections to exact geometric tolerances and mirror-finishes.

  • Micro-grinding & Polishing
  • Exact Tolerance Machining
  • Surface Preparation for Micro-Analysis
