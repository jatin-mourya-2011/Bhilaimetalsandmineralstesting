Our Operational Philosophy

We operate under strict, military-grade organizational frameworks. By adhering to the 5S Methodology (Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain), we maintain an immaculate, highly efficient laboratory environment that prevents cross-contamination.

Our Result-Oriented Process Flow is embedded into the very walls of our facility. We map every variable—from precise calibration and traceability (PM) to man, machine, material, and method—guaranteeing 100% valid outputs in strict compliance with ISO/IEC 17025:2017 requirements.

Technical Capabilities

Detailed analytical services using state-of-the-art laboratory equipment.

100 Ton Universal Testing Machine

At the heart of our mechanical testing facility sits the massive 100 Ton FIE Universal Testing Machine. Designed specifically for heavy industry, this robust equipment executes high-capacity testing on large-scale industrial metals and construction materials, pushing them to their absolute breaking points.

  • 100-Ton High-Capacity Tensile Testing
  • Heavy Industrial Compression Analysis
  • Proof Stress & Yield Point Evaluation
  • Transverse & Bending Strength Verification

Chemical Analysis (OES)

Equipped with state-of-the-art Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES), we conduct highly accurate chemical composition analysis of solid metals. This allows our analysts to rapidly quantify elemental breakdowns to verify material grades and detect trace impurities.

  • Precise Elemental Breakdown
  • Alloy Grade Verification
  • Trace Element Detection
  • Rapid Digital Readouts

Profile Projector (Dimensional)

Our advanced Profile Projector enables high-precision optical measurement and dimensional inspection of complex mechanical parts. By highly magnifying the profile of the specimen, we ensure exact geometric dimensioning and tolerancing (GD&T) compliance.

  • High-Precision Optical Measurement
  • Complex Surface Profiling
  • GD&T Tolerance Verification

Brinell Hardness Testing

Material durability and wear resistance are paramount in engineering. Using our dedicated Brinell hardness testing apparatus, we accurately evaluate the resistance of your heavy metals and castings to permanent indentation under massive loads.

  • Heavy Load Indentation Testing
  • Casting and Forging Durability
  • Surface Wear Resistance Verification

Rockwell Hardness Testing

For rapid and precise hardness evaluations on smaller components and varied alloys, our Rockwell Hardness Tester delivers immediate, highly accurate depth-penetration data, ensuring your materials meet specific engineering grades.

  • Rapid Indentation Depth Analysis
  • Multi-Scale Hardness Evaluation
  • Alloy Surface Grading

Impact Analysis

How does your material react to a sudden, violent collision? Our dedicated pendulum impact testing bay determines the energy absorption characteristics and fracture toughness of materials under extreme, sudden stress.

  • Charpy V-Notch Testing
  • Izod Impact Testing
  • Fracture Toughness Evaluation

Microstructural Analysis

Imperfections aren't always visible to the naked eye. Our high-magnification optical microscopes allow our metallurgists to examine grain structures, identify phases, and detect microscopic inclusions within metal alloys.

  • Grain Size Measurement
  • Micro-flaw & Crack Detection
  • Phase & Alloy Composition Verification

Precision Sample Preparation

Accurate diagnostics demand absolutely flawless samples. Our preparation bay utilizes dedicated polishing machines specifically calibrated for micro-samples, preparing metal cross-sections to exact geometric tolerances and mirror-finishes.

  • Micro-sample Polishing
  • Exact Tolerance Machining
  • Surface Preparation for Micro-Analysis

Sample Registration

Absolute precision starts the moment materials enter our facility. We utilize an isolated, dedicated Sample Receiving Cell to securely log, tag, and trace every single specimen, ensuring zero cross-contamination and perfect chain-of-custody.

  • Secure Specimen Logging
  • Isolated Contamination Control
  • Strict Chain-of-Custody Tracking

Digital Data Acquisition

Raw power requires absolute precision. Our testing bays are connected to advanced computerized consoles that capture micro-second data points during material failure, generating automated, highly accurate stress-strain analytics.

  • Real-Time Load Tracking
  • Automated Stress-Strain Curve Generation
  • NABL-Compliant Data Logging
